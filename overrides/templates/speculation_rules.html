{# Native Speculation Rules API. Two tiers, no JS: - prefetch (eagerness "moderate"): browser fetches the HTML for any internal href on hover / long-press. Cheap (~one HTML download per hover), and clicks land on already-cached pages. - prerender (default eagerness, kicks in immediately): full background render of the previous/next page since those are the most likely next destination from any doc page. Heaviest, but only two candidates — the cost is bounded. Browser-deduplicated, respects Cache-Control, falls back silently in browsers that don't ship the API. The .external escape hatch lets inline links opt out via class="external". #}
